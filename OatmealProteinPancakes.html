Oatmeal Protein Pancakes

How come that shredded oatmeal works?

One day I didn't want to use flour so I shredded oatmeal as a replacement.
To my surprise: it worked just fine. Of course it tastes different but these pancakes
do deserve to be named heaven-like.

But enough with the chit-chat: let's cut to the chase!

How do we get the oatmeal shredded?

No problem, I got you. You either use a common blender and blend until you have oatmeal flour.
If you are that fortunate use a Thermomix. You can blend or grind to your liking.

Ingredients for on pancake batter:

  • 220g of your oatmeal flour
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 250g milk
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 70g of proteinpowder with a flavor of your liking
  • oil for the pan, preferably coconut-oil
  1. Shred the oatmeal til its powdery.
  2. Add the bananas and milk and puree.
  3. Add cinnamon, eggs and proteinpowder and mix everything together.
  4. Best case: let it rest in the fridge for 20-30 minutes, If you want extra fluffy pancakes -
    then add a little bit of sparkling water. Trust me!
  5. Now put your stove on medium heat and put in the coconut-oil. When temperature is reached,
    add 2-3 tablespoons and let it bake. After flipping put a lid on the pan. 2 minutes on each side should suffice.
